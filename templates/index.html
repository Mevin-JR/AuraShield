Chemical Structure Recognition (CSR) System

In this era of advanced technology and automation, information extraction has become a very common practice. OCR is not adequate for processing chemical compounds as it can be represented both as textual as well as graphical.
The CSR system is a modern image processing library which allows us to extract structural information of a chemical structure.

Although this proposed system is primarily intended to help chemists complete their chemical structure and predict new chemical structures, it is also envisioned to be utilized as a learning aid for users.
